Final Summary Statement

SmartRail successfully integrates Reactive UI with Adaptive Machine Learning (Rasa) to solve the transparency problem in railway bookings. The implementation of specialized seat-grouping logic—catering to varied coach types like 1A, 2A, 3A, and SL—provides a state-of-the-art visual experience that significantly reduces booking friction. The TTE module further enhances operational efficiency by digitizing the on-board manifest and incident reporting workflow.
